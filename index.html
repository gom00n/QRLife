QRLife

Turn any link or text into a QR code, then watch it come alive in Conway's Game of Life

A tiny universe on a grid. Every cell is alive or dead, and on each step four rules decide its fate: a live cell stays alive with 2–3 live neighbours, otherwise it dies (loneliness or overcrowding); a dead cell with exactly 3 live neighbours is born. From these simple rules, patterns drift, blink, and travel — like the gliders above.
